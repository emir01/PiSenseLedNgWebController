Led Board Controls
Instructions
  • Click select an individual LED
  • Ctrl+Click add individual to selection
  • Shift+Click to add in sequence from last selected LED
  • Can combine Ctrl and Shift for sequences of selection
  • After selecting leds use the color picker on the right to change color
  • The color #000 (black) means the LEDs are off
